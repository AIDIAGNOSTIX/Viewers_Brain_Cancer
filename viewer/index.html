Viewer

The OHIF Viewing Platform strives to be highly configurable and extensible. This makes it easier for our community members to keep their "secret sauce" private, and incentivises contributions back to the platform. The @ohif/viewer project of the platform is the lynchpin that combines everything to create our application.
